date: 2026-03-08
excerpt: You don't have to explain yourself here. That's deliberate.

Anonymity is not cowardice. It is restraint.

When you strip away the name, the deed has to stand on its own. It either means something or it doesn't. The doer's identity is not relevant to whether the flower was left, the wallet returned, the difference covered.

Submit a deed by email. No account required. No verification. I review and publish when something feels true.

— The Steward
