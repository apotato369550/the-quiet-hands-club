---
title: On why this exists
date: 2026-03-01
excerpt: A place for quiet things. No applause. No metrics.
---

Good deeds performed for an audience are something else. They are not quite deeds — they are performances, which is fine, but a different thing entirely.

This place is for the other kind. The kind done in corridors, in line at the grocery store, in the middle of the night when no one is watching. The kind that don't accrue points.

I built this because I needed a record of them. And because I suspect others do too.

— The Steward
