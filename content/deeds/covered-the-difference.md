title: Covered the difference
date: 2026-02-14
excerpt: The man ahead of me was short. I paid the rest without drawing attention to it.
---

He was counting coins at the register. A few dollars short for his groceries. The line behind us was growing impatient in the way lines do.

I slid my card forward before it became a scene. Told the cashier to put the remainder on mine. Quietly. The man ahead never knew.

That's the point.
