date: 2026-02-28
excerpt: Found it on the platform. Turned it in. Didn't check inside.

Tucked between the bench slats on the eastbound platform. Leather, worn at the corners. I didn't open it.

Handed it to the station attendant. She seemed surprised. I kept walking.

There's a version of this story where I'm searching for some form of gratitude. This is not that version.
