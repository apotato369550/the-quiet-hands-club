---
title: Left flowers for a stranger
date: 2026-03-10
excerpt: A small bouquet, anonymous, left at a door where I'd heard crying the night before.
---

I don't know her name. I heard her through the wall one evening — not dramatically, just the quiet kind of crying that doesn't expect to be heard.

The next morning I left a small bunch of yellow freesias outside her door. No note. I took the stairs so I wouldn't be seen leaving.

I don't know if she found them. I don't need to know.
