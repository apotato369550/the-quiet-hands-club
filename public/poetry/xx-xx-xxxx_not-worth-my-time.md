Not worth my time.

As patience runs thin

in the court self made

As words turn the atmosphere hot

As arguments raise the temperature

AS testimonies and accounts built on character non-existential

As files and documents are brought, read, and referenced

the weight of what they contain weighing down

to all those present in the courtroom

the judge bangs his gavel

the prosecutor and defense argue

plaintiff and defendant helpless,

at the mercy of those that say they know the law.

Jury witnessing

At the end of the day, was there really a need to convene?

Over a parking ticket?

Pay the fine, and walk away.
