Playing with Puzzles

An unfinished puzzle on a desk that you really need to use

Screaming and whispering at you to work on it

Lest all your effort be for naught

Lest you betray the trust of the person that started it

With noone else to point the blame, the pieces begrudgingly fall into place

With rapid fingers, a mind that hungers, and a heart that is absent

You moved heaven and earth to make the stars align - and you did!

It's what you wanted after all, wasn't it?

So what do I feel so unsure?
